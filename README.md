# questioning

## usage

```elixir
> Questioning.describe()
Please read the next sentence and answer the problem.
Q. Who is the author of "The Tale of Genji"?
A. Shikibu Murasaki.
Exactly!

> Questioning.choise()
Please read the question and choose the right one from the options.
Q. Who was the first Shogun of the Edo Shogunate?
1. Ieyasu Tokugawa
2. Nobunaga Oda
A. 2
Try again!
```
